Understanding the Termite Problem

Termites live underground or inside wooden structures and feed on cellulose-based materials such as wood, paper, and cardboard. Due to their hidden nature, termite infestations often remain unnoticed until significant damage has already occurred. Common signs of termite infestation include hollow-sounding wood, mud tubes on walls, damaged furniture, peeling paint, and discarded wings near doors and windows.
