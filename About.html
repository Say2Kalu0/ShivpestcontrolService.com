Why Pest Control Is Essential

Pests are more than just a nuisance. They pose serious health risks, contaminate food, damage property, and affect overall hygiene. Cockroaches spread bacteria and allergens, termites silently destroy wooden structures, bed bugs disrupt sleep and mental health, rodents cause electrical damage, and mosquitoes transmit life-threatening diseases.
