Cockroaches are among the most persistent and unhygienic pests found in residential and commercial environments. Understanding the Cockroach Problem: Cockroaches thrive in warm, dark, and moist environments such as kitchens, bathrooms, drains, storage areas, and electrical ducts. Due to their nocturnal behavior, infestations often remain unnoticed until they reach advanced levels. Cockroaches multiply rapidly, making professional intervention essential for complete and long-term control.

Common Types of Cockroaches Found Indoors

  • German Cockroaches
  • American Cockroaches
  • Oriental Cockroaches

Each species requires a targeted treatment approach to ensure effective elimination and prevention of reinfestation.

Health Risks Associated with Cockroach Infestation

Cockroaches contaminate food and surfaces by carrying harmful bacteria and pathogens from drains, garbage areas, and sewers. Their presence may cause food poisoning, gastrointestinal infections, and allergic reactions. In sensitive individuals, especially children and the elderly, cockroach allergens can trigger asthma and respiratory problems.

Importance of Professional Cockroach Control
